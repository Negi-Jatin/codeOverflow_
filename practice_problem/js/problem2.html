Area of Triangle(by Heron's formula).

Problem: Find the area of triangle where length of the three of its sides are 5,6 and 7.

Solution: We can calculate and write the code for the same by using Heron's formula which says area is the square root of product of semi perimeter and difference of each side and semi perimeter. where
s=(a+b+c)/2
Area=(s*(s-a)*(s-b)*(s-c))^0.5
